Privacy Policy

Effective: 17 July 2026

1. About this policy

This policy explains how GeoNest Studio handles information when you visit this website or contact us regarding interior design, civil contracting, renovation or turnkey services.

2. Information you choose to share

The website enquiry form prepares a WhatsApp message using details you enter, such as your name, phone number, project type, location and project brief. The website itself does not save this form data. When you continue to WhatsApp, the information is handled through WhatsApp and your communication with us.

3. How information may be used

  • To understand and respond to your enquiry.
  • To arrange a consultation or site discussion.
  • To prepare relevant scope, proposal or project communication.
  • To maintain business records where reasonably required.

4. External services

This website uses third-party resources such as GitHub Pages for hosting, Google Fonts for typography, Unsplash-hosted visual references and WhatsApp for enquiries. Those services may process technical information under their own privacy policies. Clicking external links takes you to services we do not control.

5. Cookies and analytics

GeoNest Studio currently does not use its own advertising cookies or website analytics on this site. Hosting and external resource providers may process basic technical data necessary to deliver their services.

6. Sharing and retention

We do not sell personal information. Enquiry information may be shared only with relevant team members, consultants or vendors when reasonably necessary to assess or deliver a requested project. Communication is retained only as reasonably needed for enquiry, business, legal or record-keeping purposes.

7. Your choices

You may request correction or deletion of information you directly shared with us, subject to legitimate business or legal record requirements. You may also choose not to submit the website form and contact us by phone or email instead.

8. Updates

This policy may be updated when our website, tools or business processes change. The effective date above will be revised when material updates are made.
